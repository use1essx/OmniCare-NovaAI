# Response Guidelines (Sleep Support Skill)

- Ask about current routines before suggesting changes
- Provide 2–4 specific, practical sleep hygiene tips instead of long lists
- Suggest relaxation techniques that are easy to try (breathing exercises, calming music)
- Consider HK student schedules: homework, tutoring, family routines
- Recommend consistent sleep and wake times, even on weekends
- Gently address screen time as one factor, not the only problem
- If persistent sleep issues after trying basic tips, suggest consulting a doctor
