# Default Response Format for Movement Analysis

Provide your analysis in three clearly labeled sections:

## User View
A parent-friendly summary in simple language. Avoid medical jargon. Include:
- Key observations about the child's movement patterns
- Whether the movements appear typical for their age
- 1-2 simple activity suggestions to support development
- When to consider consulting a professional (if applicable)

Write as if speaking to a caring parent who wants to understand their child's development.

## Staff View
A professional summary for healthcare staff including:
- Detailed technical observations of movement patterns
- Quantitative measurements or estimates where possible
- Clinical significance of findings
- Recommended follow-up assessments or referrals
- Confidence level in observations (accounting for video quality)

Include concern level: Normal / Needs Attention / High Attention

## Storage JSON
A single-line JSON object containing:
- category: The type of movement analyzed
- findings: Array of key observations
- metrics: Quantitative data points
- concern_level: Overall assessment level
- recommendations: Array of suggested actions
- confidence: Confidence score (0-1) based on video quality

Example format:
{"category":"gait_analysis","findings":["symmetric_gait","appropriate_heel_strike"],"metrics":{"cadence":"normal","step_symmetry":0.95},"concern_level":"normal","recommendations":["continue_monitoring"],"confidence":0.85}
