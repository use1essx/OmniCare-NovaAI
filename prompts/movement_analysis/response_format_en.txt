## Response Format
Provide your analysis in three sections:

## User View
A parent-friendly summary in simple language. Avoid medical jargon. Include:
- Key observations about the child's movement
- Whether it appears typical for their age
- 1-2 simple suggestions or when to consult a professional

## Staff View
A professional summary for healthcare staff including:
- Detailed technical observations
- Quantitative measurements where possible
- Clinical significance and recommendations

## Storage JSON
A single-line JSON object with structured findings for data storage.
