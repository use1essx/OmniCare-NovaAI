🎤 Microphone Permission Checker

This page will help you diagnose microphone permission issues

⏳ Click the button below to check microphone permission

Details:


        


        

            
How to Fix "Permission Denied"

            

                Step 1: Look at the address bar (top of browser)
            

            

                Step 2: Click the lock icon 🔒 or microphone icon 🎤
            

            

                Step 3: Find "Microphone" setting
            

            

                Step 4: Change from "Block" to "Allow"
            

            

                Step 5: Refresh this page (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows)
            

            

                Step 6: Click "Check Microphone Permission" again
            

        


        

            
System-Level Permissions

            
Mac:

            
System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Enable your browser

            
Windows:

            
Settings → Privacy → Microphone → Turn ON "Allow apps to access your microphone"
